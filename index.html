The Moment

The world is changing.
We have been here before.

In 1906, seven men at Cornell University looked at a world that was not designed for their success and decided to build something that was. Alpha Phi Alpha was born from that conviction — not as a response to the moment, but as an answer to it.

We are in that kind of moment again. Artificial intelligence is reshaping every sector — education, civic engagement, professional life, and community service. The question is not whether it will affect the communities we serve. It is whether our chapter will meet this moment with the same clarity of purpose that defined our founding.

"The brothers of Eta Zeta Lambda are educators, executives, lawyers, physicians, and engineers — men whose lifelong expertise is exactly what AI amplifies, never what it replaces."

Brother Morris raised this opportunity. Brother President Parris connected it to our voter registration work. This proposal is a response to both of you — and to the chapter's long tradition of meeting consequential moments with action.

The Proposal

A 90-minute workshop that
changes what brothers believe is possible.

Not a lecture. Not a PowerPoint. Brothers watch functional tools built live, from natural language, in real time — then take those tools home and use them immediately.

01
Live Comparison

Three AI platforms run simultaneously on the same prompt. Brothers see the differences. The session begins with demonstration, not instruction.

02
Voteless People Tool

Brothers input any Westchester zip code and receive a complete picture of local offices, ballot issues, and why each race affects their neighborhood. Built live.

03
Chapter Flyer Builder

Upload prior chapter flyers, provide event details, and AI generates four design-ready options instantly. Brothers leave with a tool they will use next week.

04
Westchester Data Walk

An interactive county-wide education equity dashboard — live AI analysis of school district data refreshed in real time. Our chapter becomes the source of truth.

The session runs on Zoom. No accounts required. No software to install. Every tool works immediately in any browser — because if it requires setup, it will not be used.
